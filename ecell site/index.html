What is E-CELL?
An Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell) is a student-run organization that promotes and supports student-led business ventures by providing resources, networking opportunities, and skill development through workshops and events. It aims to foster innovation and entrepreneurial thinking among students.
Our Mission
Builing a thriving entrepreneurial ecosystem through empowering students community to unleash their creative potential & Nurturing Innovations
